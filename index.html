About Us

Seyler Enterprises, Inc. is owned and operated by Gerald and Amy Seyler, a family‑oriented business located in Jersey Shore, Pennsylvania. Jerry is actively involved in the natural stone industry and is a standing member of the Allied Stone Industries (ASI).

Our focus is to consistently provide high‑quality stone products for our clients. We are committed to continuous improvement and to offering a variety of products suitable for both interior and exterior applications.

From initial selection through shipment, we strive to deliver a completely satisfying experience. In addition to our local products, we offer numerous building and landscaping stones sourced from across the United States, as well as China and India.
